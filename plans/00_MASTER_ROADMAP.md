# Master Roadmap: WT-Coach Evolution

This document serves as the strategic orchestrator for the three major campaigns to evolve the WT-Coach platform into a Modular Monolith with advanced Mapping and Nutrition capabilities.

## 🗺️ Strategic Pillars

### [Campaign 1: Modular Monolith Refactor](./01_modular_monolith_refactor.md)
*   **Goal:** Restructure the frontend into a Feature-Driven architecture.
*   **Outcome:** A scalable, organized codebase where Maps and Nutrition can live in isolation without polluting the core logic.
*   **Status:** 🟥 Not Started

### [Campaign 2: Maps, Heatmap & Route Builder](./02_maps_and_route_builder.md)
*   **Goal:** Integrate the `../tcx` geospatial engine for route planning and trail discovery.
*   **Outcome:** A historical heatmap of all rides and a "Stitcher" to build new routes from previous segments.
*   **Status:** 🟥 Not Started

### [Campaign 3: Nutrition & Multimodal AI Coach](./03_nutrition_and_ai_coach.md)
*   **Goal:** Implement meal tracking and energy balance using the ADK AI Coach.
*   **Outcome:** AI-powered meal photo analysis and advice that correlates intake (macros) with expenditure (actual ride data).
*   **Status:** 🟥 Not Started

## 🚀 Execution Sequence

1.  **Phase 1 (Refactor):** Prepare the ground. We cannot add complex features to a disorganized core.
2.  **Phase 2 (Maps):** Build the geospatial foundation. This is the most technically complex logic.
3.  **Phase 3 (Nutrition):** Build the data-driven coaching loop. This ties everything together into a holistic training assistant.
